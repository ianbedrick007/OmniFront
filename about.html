The Company

Building the Future of African Commerce.

OmniLabs AI Solutions is a Ghanaian technology startup building AI-powered business automation tools for small and medium enterprises (SMEs).

Our Mission

In Africa, small businesses are the backbone of the economy. However, many struggle to provide 24/7 customer engagement and seamless payment processing.

We build "Always Awake" AI assistants that live where your customers are—on WhatsApp and Voice—enabling merchants to scale without limits.
